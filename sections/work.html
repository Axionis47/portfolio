Graph neural networks

Session-Based GNN Recommendations

A graph recommender for shoppers with no history: it trusts the session, a co-occurrence graph of 172,066 past sessions, and nothing else. Cutting the feed-forward layers to fit a $300 course budget made the transformer faster to train and more accurate — 38.28% validation Recall@10 across 82,173 items, 2.6× the GraphSAGE baseline.

Semantic search on a small budget. Distilled a 560M cross-encoder into a 33M bi-encoder using KL-divergence and three stages of hard-negative mining: about 97% of the teacher’s quality, 100× faster, roughly $4 to train.
